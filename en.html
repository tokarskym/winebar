wine bar

Republika Wina is an intimate place in the very center of Warsaw, which combines two concepts - a wine bar and a wine shop. You will find over 400 carefully selected wines from all over the world, mainly from small producers, but also famous Chateaux from the French Bordeaux region, often in vintages that are no longer available on the market. As promoters of our native winemaking, we have also created an extensive selection of Polish wines.

menu

We try to make every visit with us full of positive experiences. That’s why we’re waiting for you with professional and hospitable service and lots of wines by the glass with specially selected selections of cheeses, cold meats, and original snacks.

book a table / events

Our restaurant has two rooms where you can organize a meeting, a special event or a professional tasting led by one from our sommeliers.

Please contact us via winebar@republikawina.pl Our manager will stay in touch with you, taking care for the uniqueness of your event.
